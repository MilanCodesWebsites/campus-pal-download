Install CampusPal on iOS

Follow these simple steps to install CampusPal on your iOS device:

Step 1

Open Safari and visit campuspal.com/download

Launch Safari on your iOS device and navigate to our download page at campuspal.com/download.

Step 2

Click "Download for iOS"

Tap the "Download for iOS" button on the download page to begin the installation process.

Step 3

Add to Home Screen

Tap the share icon at the bottom of the screen, then scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

Step 4

Open and Start Using

Tap the CampusPal icon on your home screen to open the app and start enjoying all its features!
